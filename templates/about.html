Our Company

Options Trades Hub Holdings is a leading Copy Trade, Options and Stocks acquisition and non-operated working interest enterprise focused on identifying untapped value on the behalf of land owners throughout the United States. Established in 2019, we are headquartered in United States with satellite offices in Europe and Asia. Our rapidly growing client list of stock owners and investors stretch across all countries.

As a family-owned company, we prioritize capital deployment and asset management projects that directly impact, and benefit, individual stock owners and investors. We combine decades of high-level investment experience with cutting-edge technology, analytically driven financial expertise, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.


Our Sale and Leasing Process

We enable investors to earn up to 10% annual yield via high-yield, low-risk stocks. Here’s how:

1. Handpick High-Yield Assets

Using powerful proprietary technology, we analyze data and identify the best stocks to strategically target for acquisition.

2. Partner with Investors

We pool our investors’ capital together to purchase new income-producing mineral assets across the New Zealand.

3. Collect Monthly Revenues

Each month, oil and gas operators pay out recurring monthly revenues for the lifespan of the mineral royalty asset.

4. Pay Out Interest to Investors

For the term of the bond, Options TH pays up to 1% daily yields in weekly and monthly payments.. Certain investments also qualify for compounding interest.

Why Invest With Options TH?
